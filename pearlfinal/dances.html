India has a very rich tradition of classical dance and 'Abhivyaktika', the solo classical dance competition, is a celebration of this tradition. 'Abhivyakti' translates into "articulation of one's feelings" and in this competition, classical dancers express various emotions through dance. The event is conducted in multiple rounds wherein the first round requires the participant to perform a prepared sequence and the subsequent rounds involve interaction with the judge. Each participant can perform any one of the classical dance forms - Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Kuchipudi, Odissi, Mohiniyattam, Manipuri, Kathakali, and Sattriya.
