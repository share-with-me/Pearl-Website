Advaita



Advaita are perhaps the most traditional of all the top rock bands in the country today, fusing together breathtaking classical musicianship with Sting-toned soft rock.

One of the four Indian bands to be selected by the legendary producer John Leckie for British Council's SoundPad, they have established themselves as one of the best live acts in the country by constantly headlining stages in premier music fests such as NH7 Weekender as well as featuring in shows like Coke Studio and MTV Unplugged.

Must listen tracks include : Mofunk, Durga, Miliha, Rasiya, Ghir Ghir, and Words.
