Pearl, the cultural extravaganza of BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus is back to steal your hearts again this year. With the exquisite alter ego: Odyssey of Legends, Pearl '15 is set to be the salvation of the year-long efforts of the enthusiastic and passionate club members of diverse fields including but not restricted to Dance, Dramatics, Photography and Literary Appreciation. Witness BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus as it transforms itself into a mythological battleground where creativity takes on indomitable spirit. Set against a legendary backdrop, Pearl in its renewed avatar brings to you an avenue for all your talents. This year we're journeying off to every world with our passions personified as art. Pearl is the manifestation of the colors of every dreamer's imagination. Pearl is that simultaneous reality that will whisk you away to those three days of pure enchantment. Pearl is all set to be the progeny of our campus culture and it is the one-stop platform where being ourselves unequivocally is the only option we're given. And this is us inviting you to unfurl the raw enigma of Pearl and be a part of this glorious tradition. Engage yourself in the ecstatic pulse of Pearl this March and sway to its rhythms and intricacies!
